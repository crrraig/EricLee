Eric Lee is an experienced architectural designer with knowledge in exterior envelope, interior design and architectural details. He has accumulated over 7 years of professional work experience in internationally recognized architectural design firms, mainely in New York City, with a primary interest in development of detailed design and corresponding technical details. He is a graduate of University of Waterloo in Canada with an honor's degree in bachelor of architectural studies. He graduated with distinction and dean's honor list in 2013 with two years of professional work experience from pretigious architecutral firms in NYC, USA, Rotterdam, Netherlands and London, UK. Since 2013, he has been practicing architecture by working in commercial architecture firms in New York City. He holds professional credentials in LEED AP BD+C and WELL AP. Most recently, He received a specialized certification in Construction Management from Columbia University with graduate courses in construction project management. He also received certifications in Project Management from University of California and Renewable Energy and Green Building Entreneurship from Duke University. In addition to his dedicated interests and experience in feasibility, sustainable and detailed design work, he also holds interest and has recent expereience in managing construction administration work. His past collaborating firms include Perkins+Will, FXCollaborative, OMA, KPF, SOM, Pei Cobb Freed & Partners, RAMSA and most recently, Beyer Blinder Belle Architects & Planners. He currently resides in New York City and is reachable by email or phone for possible future collaboration.
