Biography:

Eric Lee is an experienced architectural professional, based in New York City, with broad work experience in internationally recognized architectural design firms. He has diverse experience in various typologies of architectural design work, including mixed-use, residential, retail, commercial office, cultural, educational and renovation projects. His experience emcompasses both exterior and interior work, and he has worked on numerous sensitive landmark projects across New York City. His major experience comes from working in internationally recognized commercial architectural design firms in New York City, in addition to experience in London, UK and Rotterdam, Netherlands. He is a graduate of University of Waterloo in Canada with an honor's degree in bachelor of architectural studies, which he graduated with distinction and dean's list. During his time at University of Waterloo, he gained plus two years of full-time professional work experience in internally recogonzied design firms around the globe. Most recently, he was a project designer and architect with Beyer Blinder Belle Architects working on numerous building typologies and scales in the Greater New York and Tri-state area, including both ground-up and renovation projects. He holds several professional credentials, including LEED AP BD+C from USGBC and WELL AP from IWBI. He is also affiliated with AIA New York Associate. Recently, he received multiple specialzied certifications from post-graduate programs: a specialized certification in Construction Management from Columbia University with the courses specializing in construction administration and management. He also received specialized certifications in Project Management from University of California, International Business from University of Colorado and and Healthier Building Materials, Affordable Housing and Sustainable Buildings from Parsons University. He is OSHA-10 certified and trained for construction industry. His past professional work experience includes Perkins+Will, KPF, FXCollaborative, Office for Metropolitan Architecture (OMA), SOM, Pei Cobb Freed & Partners, Robert A.M. Stern Architects and Beyer Blinder Belle Architects & Planners. His experience, interest and knowledge expands to all phases of architectural project from feasibility design studies to construction administration. In addition to his well-rounded professional background, he has a keen interest in sustainable and real estate development. By using his unique knowledge of architectural design and construction, his work approach emphasizes attention to detail and collaboration.
